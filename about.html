About Us

Nawah — Building Syria's Digital Future

Our Story

Nawah started from a clear vision: to put Syria at the forefront of Arab countries in the digital space. We are a team of ambitious Syrian youth who believe technology is the key to a better future. We started as a small idea — a nucleus — and today we build digital projects that leave a real impact on the Syrian market. Every project we build reflects our passion for excellence and our belief that a great digital product starts from a solid foundation.

🔭

Our Vision

We aspire to be Syria's number one digital company, transforming how Syrian businesses engage with technology — from a mere tool to a strategic partner in growth and success.

🎯

Our Mission

Empowering Syrian businesses and stores through innovative, professional digital solutions tailored to local market needs — with global standards in quality and design.

We are at the beginning of a long journey with unlimited ambition. Our goals include:

  • To become Syria's number one digital company within the next five years
  • Expand our services to include AI and machine learning

  • Contribute to developing Syria's digital infrastructure
  • Build strategic partnerships with Syrian startups and institutions
